Our Story

Where it began

ShelfSpace began in late nights between classes, when I stacked dog-eared paperbacks beside glowing screens at the University of South Carolina. I wanted one warm digital home to track stories, share joy, and connect readers across campuses, couches, and countries together.

Interests and hobbies

ShelfSpace Book Clubs bring readers together beyond the pages. Whether meeting at a cozy cafe or connecting online, members share ideas, swap recommendations, and experience stories as a community. It's a space where different perspectives meet, conversations flow, and a shared love of books turns casual readers into lifelong literary friends.
